What to do if a diagnosis of the botulism occurs ?

Patients suspected of having botulism should immediately be placed under intensive care and administered :

  • A botulinum antitoxin.
  • Supportive care, including mechanical ventilation if necessary.The antitoxin is more effective if administered within 24 hours of the onset of symptoms.

In cases of botulism caused by inoculation, the woundshould be debrided and drained surgically. Antibiotics, usually penicillin, will be administered.totherdiseases of the central nervous system.
