An exclusive partnership to fight against Botulism
Our Vision

The partnership between Biomed Warsaw, a leading European manufacturer of botulism serum, and BioDrug Ltd., a pharmaceutical company specialised in distribution & commercialisation of vaccines and immunoglobulins, strengthens our collective ability to prevent and treat botulism in Europe.

Our collaboration stand for :

  • Promoting the unique European producer of ABE Botulism serum
  • Accessibility of serum at affordable price
  • Self dependency of Europe in the treatment of rare diseases.
  • Extending the commercial presence of Biomed using the network of Biodrug

For more information, please contact us: botulism@biodrug.sk
