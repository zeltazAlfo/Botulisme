An exclusive partnership to fight against Botulism
Our Vision

The partnership between Biomed Warsaw, a leading European manufacturer of botulism serum, and BioDrug Ltd., a pharmaceutical company specialised in distribution & commercialisation of vaccines and immunoglobulins, strengthens our collective ability to prevent and treat botulism in Europe.

Our collaboration stand for :

  • Promoting the unique European producer of ABE Botulism serum
  • Accessibility of serum at affordable price
  • Self dependency of Europe in the treatment of rare diseases.
  • Extending the commercial presence of Biomed using the network of Biodrug

Our Roles & Expertise
Bio Drug specialises in the commercialization, distribution, and promotion of medical treatments, with a strong focus on the CSE region. Leveraging an extensive network of healthcare partners and medical institutions, the company ensures wide accessibility to innovative therapeutic solutions, fostering increased awareness and facilitating market penetration across diverse regional markets.
Biomed Warsaw is a leading and highly regarded entity in the pharmaceutical industry, renowned for its specialization in the production of biological active ingredients (API) and medicinal products (antitoxin serums, biological vaccines). With decades of cutting-edge research and development, the company delivers treatments that adhere to the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficacy.
